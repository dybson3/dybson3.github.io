Cybersecurity Resources

A curated list of tools, articles, and materials for blue teaming, incident response, and general security.

Learning Platforms

  • 1. TryHackMe ⭐ Top Pick The absolute best place to start. Incredible interactive rooms covering both offensive and defensive security (SOC Level 1 path is highly recommended).
  • 2. LetsDefend Excellent Blue Team training. Simulates a real SOC environment where you investigate alerts, analyze logs, and perform incident response.
  • 3. HackTheBox (HTB Academy) More advanced platform, excellent for deep-diving into specific vulnerabilities and getting hands-on with pentesting and advanced defense.

Training & Labs

  • 1. CyberDefenders ⭐ Top Pick Incredible blue team platform offering free labs focusing on digital forensics, incident response (DFIR), and threat hunting.
  • 2. Google Cybersecurity Certificate A great foundational course for absolute beginners wanting to understand the basics of networks, Linux, Python, and SIEMs.
  • 3. Antisyphon Training High-quality, affordable (often "Pay What You Can") training courses from industry experts like John Strand.

Tools & Reference

  • 1. MITRE ATT&CK ⭐ Top Pick The global knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques. Essential for every SOC analyst to map threats.
  • 2. CyberChef The "Cyber Swiss Army Knife". A simple web app for decoding, analyzing, and converting data (Base64, HEX, malware deobfuscation).
  • 3. GTFOBins & LOLBAS Curated lists of legitimate binaries that can be abused by attackers to bypass security restrictions on Linux and Windows.
