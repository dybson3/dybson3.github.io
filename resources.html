Cybersecurity Resources

A curated list of tools, articles, and materials for blue teaming, incident response, and general security.

Learning Platforms

  • TryHackMe ⭐ Top Pick The absolute best place to start. Incredible interactive rooms covering both offensive and defensive security.
  • LetsDefend Excellent Blue Team training. Simulates a real SOC environment where you investigate alerts and analyze logs.
  • HackTheBox (HTB) More advanced platform, excellent for deep-diving into specific vulnerabilities and hands-on pentesting.
  • Web Security Academy Free, world-class web application security training from the creators of Burp Suite.

Training & Labs

  • CyberDefenders ⭐ Top Pick Incredible blue team platform offering free labs focusing on digital forensics, incident response (DFIR).
  • Google Cybersecurity A great foundational course for absolute beginners wanting to understand the basics of networks, Linux, Python.
  • Antisyphon Training High-quality, affordable (often "Pay What You Can") training courses from industry experts like John Strand.
  • RangeForce Community Offers a free community edition with great interactive SOC analyst and cloud security modules.

YouTube Channels

  • John Hammond ⭐ Top Pick Legendary content creator covering malware analysis, CTFs, and real-world vulnerabilities.
  • NetworkChuck High-energy networking and security basics. Great for getting hyped about learning IT.
  • David Bombal Deep technical interviews with industry giants and practical networking/security tutorials.
  • Gerald Auger (Simply Cyber) Amazing resource specifically tailored for breaking into the GRC and SOC side of cybersecurity.

Tools & Reference

  • MITRE ATT&CK ⭐ Top Pick The global knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques. Essential for every SOC analyst to map threats.
  • CyberChef The "Cyber Swiss Army Knife". A simple web app for decoding, analyzing, and converting data.
  • GTFOBins & LOLBAS Curated lists of legitimate binaries that can be abused by attackers to bypass security restrictions.
  • VirusTotal Analyze suspicious files, domains, IPs and URLs to detect malware and other breaches.

News & Threat Intel

  • BleepingComputer ⭐ Top Pick The most reliable source for up-to-date cybersecurity news, ransomware tracking, and vulnerabilities.
  • The Hacker News Widely read cybersecurity news source covering breaches, malware, and exploits.
  • Krebs on Security In-depth investigative journalism by Brian Krebs focusing on cybercrime and security.
